Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I distribute my invoices from the app? After completing the final step and entering the invoice date, the app opens the device share sheet. From there you can print, email, message, or choose another available option to send your invoice.
  • How do I delete old invoices or information I no longer want to see? Swipe left on any invoice, address entry, or payment item to reveal the delete option.
  • How do I change the invoice number shown on generated invoices? Open Settings from the main page and update the next invoice number there.
  • Where can I find the privacy policy, terms and conditions, or license information? This information is available in the app’s Settings section.
